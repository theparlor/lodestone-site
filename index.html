Awareness & record

Your whole stack, searchable, on your own machine.

Lodestone is a local semantic-recall engine. Ask in plain language — find what you actually built. Memory entries, Cast personas, library catalog, Intent signals: all indexed, all queryable, none of it leaving your machine. No cloud. No API key. No third-party seeing your second brain.

The problem it solves

You built it. You can't find it. That's the tax.

Every AI surface accumulates state — memories written, personas synthesized, decisions recorded, signals logged. None of that state was previously reachable across surfaces. You'd ask Claude, get something generic. Ask again in a different session, get a different answer. Lodestone eliminates the recall gap: it indexes everything into a single local vector store and returns ranked, source-cited hits in under a second. The system stops being amnesiac.

798 records ingested. 41 tests passing. Real recall against live memory, in the time it takes to finish typing the question.
How you use it

Ingest once. Ask anything.

1

Ingest your corpora

One command pulls in memory, Cast personas, library-index catalog, and Intent signals. The index lives at ~/.context/lodestone/ — on disk, local, yours.

2

Ask in plain language

No query syntax. No field names. Just the question: "how do we handle commit autonomy in a solo repo?" Lodestone ranks hits by cosine similarity and returns the source slug.

3

Read the citations back

Every result names its source corpus and record. You know whether the answer came from your own memory, a persona synthesis, a catalogued artifact, or a signal log — and can follow the breadcrumb.

What makes it different

The conservation law: recall never writes back.

The rule the whole product is built around

The index is read-only. The corpus is never touched.

Lodestone is the READ sibling to Witness's write-spine. Every query is a projection — the index on disk is byte-identical before and after any recall call. The product can't corrupt the record it's reading, by construction. This is enforced at the test layer (test_recall_projection.py) and held as an invariant across the stack. The two channels it exposes are structurally separate:

Ranked hits

Cosine-scored matches from any corpus — returned with source slug, score, and a content excerpt the user can act on.

Surgical recall

Precision mode that targets a single corpus or record type — for when you know the domain and want signal, not breadth.
